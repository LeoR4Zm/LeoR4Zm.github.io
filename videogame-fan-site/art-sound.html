Visuals & audio

Art direction and soundtracks

The look and sound of a game define its mood long before the player understands the story.

Art style

Games can be realistic, stylized, pixel-based, hand-drawn, or abstract. The style is chosen to support the tone of the game and the limits of the hardware.

  • Color palettes communicate emotion and atmosphere
  • Camera angles and composition guide player attention
  • UI design influences clarity and immersion

Music & sound design

Soundtracks and sound effects shape how players feel about every moment. Quiet ambient tracks can create tension, while strong themes become iconic memories attached to a game.

  • Dynamic music can change based on player actions
  • Audio cues signal danger, rewards, or hidden secrets
  • Voice acting brings characters to life
