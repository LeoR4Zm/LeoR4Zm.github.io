Players & participation

Gaming community & culture

Modern games are social spaces. Players share strategies, create art, tell stories, and sometimes build entire careers around their favorite titles.

Streaming & content creation

Streaming platforms let players broadcast their gameplay and interact with viewers in real time. This turns games into performances and shared experiences.

  • Live chat connects streamers and audiences
  • Clips and highlight reels spread moments across social media
  • Some creators build long-term communities around specific games

eSports & competition

Competitive scenes organize tournaments, leagues, and world championships. Fans follow teams and players much like traditional sports.

  • Team-based and 1v1 formats across many genres
  • Professional players, analysts, and commentators
  • Large events that combine live audiences and online viewing

Fan creations

Fans extend games beyond their original design through mods, fan art, guides, music remixes, roleplay communities, and more.

  • Mods can add new mechanics, quests, or visual styles
  • Fan fiction and fan art explore characters in new ways
  • Community-run wikis act as living encyclopedias
