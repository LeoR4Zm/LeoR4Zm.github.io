Players & participation

Gaming community & culture

Modern gaming is social. Players share strategies, create fan art, stream their gameplay, and sometimes build entire careers around games.

Streaming & content creation

Platforms allow players to broadcast gameplay and build audiences. This turns games into performances and shared experiences.

eSports and competition

Competitive scenes organize tournaments, leagues, and world championships. Many players enjoy following teams the same way others follow sports clubs.

Fan creations

Fans often extend games beyond their original design by making mods, fan fiction, fan art, remixes, and guides. These creations show how deeply games can affect people.
