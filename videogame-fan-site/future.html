Looking ahead

The future of video games

Technology will continue to change how games look and feel, but the core remains the same: interactive experiences where players make meaningful choices.

New technologies

Advancements such as virtual reality, cloud gaming, and AI-driven systems may allow for more dynamic worlds and characters that react in complex ways.

Diversity & accessibility

A major part of the future of games is social: who gets represented, who gets to make games, and who can play them. Accessibility features and inclusive design help more players take part.
