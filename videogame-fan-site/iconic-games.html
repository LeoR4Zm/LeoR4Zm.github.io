Iconic games & awards

Some games become famous for changing how we play, how we think about stories, or how the industry works.

What makes a game “iconic”?

A game can be iconic because of its mechanics, its worldbuilding, its soundtrack, or even the communities that form around it. Awards and critical reviews often highlight these innovations, but players also decide what becomes a classic.

  • Introducing new game mechanics or control schemes
  • Setting a new visual or narrative standard
  • Inspiring many later games or fan communities
