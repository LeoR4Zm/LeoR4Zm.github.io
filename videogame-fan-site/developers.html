People behind the pixels

Developers & studios

Every game is built by teams of artists, programmers, writers, designers, and more. Different studios build different identities and strengths over time.

Large studios

Big studios often work on multi-year projects with hundreds of staff. They create large-scale experiences and series that become well-known franchises.

Indie teams

Smaller teams can experiment with unusual ideas and personal stories. Many indie games become influential even with limited budgets.

Roles on a game team

Game development involves many roles: designers, programmers, artists, animators, level designers, sound designers, QA testers, producers, and more.
