Stir-fried Tomato and Scrambled Eggs

A well-known and beloved traditional Chinese dish. Easy to cook, colorful, and comforting — perfect with rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium tomatoes (about 100 g)
  • 4 eggs (about 200 g)
  • 2 g salt
  • 4–6 g sugar
  • 3–5 g vegetable oil (or any cooking oil)

Directions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl until smooth. Chop the tomatoes into small pieces.
  2. Heat a frying pan or wok over medium heat. Add all the oil.
  3. Pour in the beaten eggs. When they begin to set, scramble lightly until fluffy. Remove and set aside.
  4. Add tomatoes to the same pan and stir-fry for 30–45 seconds. Add salt and sugar, cook until juicy.
  5. Return eggs, mix well with the tomatoes, and serve hot.

Notes

Adjust sugar and salt to taste. The dish should balance sweet and savory flavors.
