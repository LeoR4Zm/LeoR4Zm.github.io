Stir-fried Tomato and Scrambled Eggs

It is a well-known and beloved traditional Chinese dish. It is very easy to cook and delicious. The ingredients are ordinary but perfectly combined.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium tomatoes (about 100 g)
  • 4 eggs (about 200 g)
  • 2 g salt
  • 4–6 g sugar
  • 3–5 g vegetable oil (or any cooking oil)

Directions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl until smooth. Chop the tomatoes into small pieces.
  2. Heat a frying pan or Chinese wok to over 275 °F (135 °C). Add all the oil.
  3. After about 15 seconds, pour in the beaten eggs. When part of the egg begins to set, stir and scramble. Cook until the eggs are fluffy, golden, and fully set. Remove and set aside.
  4. Add the tomatoes to the same pan and stir-fry for 30–45 seconds. Add salt and sugar, then continue stir-frying for 1 more minute.
  5. Return the eggs to the pan, mix well with the tomatoes, and serve hot.
